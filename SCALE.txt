SCALE CONVERSIONS
=================

Units used in the simulation:

MASS: 1 unit = 10^24 kg
-----
Sun:     1989000.0 units = 1.989 × 10^30 kg
Jupiter: 1898.0 units    = 1.898 × 10^27 kg
Earth:   5.97 units      = 5.97 × 10^24 kg
Venus:   4.87 units      = 4.87 × 10^24 kg
Mars:    0.64 units      = 6.4 × 10^23 kg
Mercury: 0.33 units      = 3.3 × 10^23 kg

DISTANCE: 1 unit = 1 million km = 10^9 meters
---------
Jupiter: 778.5 units  = 778.5 million km = 5.2 AU
Mars:    227.9 units  = 227.9 million km = 1.52 AU
Earth:   149.6 units  = 149.6 million km = 1.0 AU
Venus:   108.2 units  = 108.2 million km = 0.72 AU
Mercury: 57.9 units   = 57.9 million km  = 0.39 AU

VELOCITY: 1 unit = 1 km/s
---------
Mercury: -47.87 units = 47.87 km/s
Venus:   -35.02 units = 35.02 km/s
Earth:   -29.78 units = 29.78 km/s
Mars:    -24.07 units = 24.07 km/s
Jupiter: -13.07 units = 13.07 km/s

GRAVITATIONAL CONSTANT: G (tuned for this scale)
-----------------------
Adjust in src/physics/settings.rs to balance orbital stability
